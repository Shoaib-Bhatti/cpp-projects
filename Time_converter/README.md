---

🌟 **What This System Can Do**
- 🔁 Convert **Seconds ↔ Minutes**  
- 🔁 Convert **Minutes ↔ Hours**  
- 🔁 Convert **Hours ↔ Days**  
- 🧭 Menu-based system with instant conversions  
- 🧮 Beginner-friendly logic  

---

🛠️ **How It Works (Simple & Clear)**
When the program starts, it shows a menu with six conversion options.  
You select your choice, enter a value, and instantly get your converted result.  

Here are the formulas behind the system:
- Minutes = Seconds ÷ 60  
- Seconds = Minutes × 60  
- Hours = Minutes ÷ 60  
- Minutes = Hours × 60  
- Days = Hours ÷ 24  
- Hours = Days × 24  

No complex math — just simple time relationships coded efficiently.
